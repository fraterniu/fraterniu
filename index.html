VALUES

To understand our values, it is important to look at the global context around us. Our Planet!

What concepts define our world today?

The term VUCA —Volatile, Uncertain, Complex, and Ambiguous— reflects the unpredictable world we live in.

To thrive, we need to adapt, understanding what we can control and what we can’t. At the same time, we must focus on core areas that shape our lives and guide our actions.

Fundamental Areas

In a constantly changing world, it’s crucial to adapt and approach every situation differently. Focusing on just one aspect of life won’t lead to true balance and growth.

To succeed and prepare for life’s challenges, we focus on five key areas:

MIND

This area focuses on expanding cognitive and emotional capacities through learning, critical thinking, and creativity, empowering individuals to make sound, ethical decisions.

BODY

This area focuses on maintaining physical health through exercise, nutrition, and rest, ensuring individuals have the energy and balance to meet life’s demands.

RESOURCES

This area focuses on mastering the management of time, tools, and natural resources. It emphasizes understanding how to efficiently use what we’ve created and what the Earth provides for sustainable growth.

VALUE

This area focuses on understanding the dynamics of prosperity—financial literacy, personal branding, and self-worth. It’s not just about money, but also about how individuals create and preserve their personal value and leverage opportunities for growth.

ENVIRONMENT

This area focuses on cultivating a healthy relationship with the natural and social environment, promoting sustainability, respect, and harmony in everyday interactions and spaces.

While the priority of each area differs for every person, it’s important to invest time in all of them for a well-rounded life.

For example, an engineer might focus on mind and resources—developing logical thinking and learning frameworks—but still needs to organize their environment, maintain health, and understand value-related aspects like financial planning or personal branding.

Even if the focus isn’t equal, acknowledging and nurturing each area ensures a more productive, fulfilling path.

MAIN PILARS

The pillars—Whole Person Development, purposeful actions, and connectedness—ensure balanced personal development, meaningful goals, and strong community relationships.

PILAR 1

Whole Person Development

This pillar focuses on the continuous development of mind, body, and spirit. It encourages members to pursue knowledge, maintain physical health, and seek spiritual meaning, ensuring a well-rounded growth.

PILAR 2

Purposeful Actions

This pillar emphasizes intention and mindfulness in every action. It encourages members to align their activities with their core values and goals, fostering a sense of purpose and fulfillment.

PILAR 3

Connectedness

This pillar is about building meaningful relationships and contributing to the community. It underlines the importance of social skills, empathy, and collaboration, recognizing that personal growth is intertwined with the well-being of others.

Unlike areas, require an equitable balance. In the areas there will always be one or some that you want to enhance more, because they relate and work better to achieve what you consider success in your life.
