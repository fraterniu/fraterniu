FRATERNI
PRINCIPLES

To understand our principles, it is important to look at the global context around us. Our Planet!

What concepts would best define our world over time?

The acronym VUCA was bestowed on our planet near the close of the 1900s because the associated terms—Volatile, Uncertain, Complex, and Ambiguous— accurately characterize our world.

We must learn to adjust to changing conditions and recognize what aspects of our lives we can and cannot control, but we must never lose sight of the fundamental areas and the central pillars of our lives.

Fundamental Areas

Keeping in mind that our world is full of change, it is really important to think different in all circumstances, since focusing on just one aspect of life does not achieve personal balance and growth.

For that reason, we think that in order to success in life, and be prepared for any challenge in life. We need to centralize our thoughts in five fundamental areas:

MIND

Sort your mental priorities to make informed decisions that improve your daily well-being.

BODY

Take care of your health to face physical challenges with energy and balance in every aspect of your life.

RESOURCES

Manage responsibly and take advantage of what you have to get ahead of challenges.

VALUE

Master your finances to plan for the long term, achieving freedom and financial security.

ENVIRONMENT

Evaluate your environment to identify improvements and adapt to current circumstances.

While the priority of each area differs for every person, it’s important to invest time in all of them for a well-rounded life.

For example, an engineer might focus heavily on mind and resources—developing logical thinking and learning frameworks—but they still need to organize their environment, maintain their health, and understand value-related aspects like financial planning or personal branding.

Even if the focus isn’t equal, acknowledging and nurturing each area ensures a more productive, fulfilling path.
